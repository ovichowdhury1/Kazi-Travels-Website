Plan Your Trip

Whether you are a visitor to South East Florida or a long time resident, A Regal Transportation Services plays a prominent role in any vacation plan. South East Florida roads are congested and the driving habits of some are unfamiliar to even scary to new travelers. It is better to use the experienced drivers from A Regal Transportation Services than to brave traffic on South East florida highways. Here are some tips to make your vacation plans run as smooth as possible

  • 1. Understand Florida Seasons. Tourist Season is considered Mid-December to Mid-April when the weather is more temperate and tourists flock from northern cities to our southern paradise. Summer is hot, humid and rainy, but is many bargains can be found if you can brave the heat.
  • 2. Depending on the season more drivers will be on the road. Florida does not have the robust transit services of major cities to the north. As a result there are many more drivers on the road than you might find in large cities.
  • 3. Plan Ahead. If you are traveling during tourist seasons, the demand for Transporation Services will be much higher, so plan ahead. Call the professionals at A Regal Transportation Services to outline your travel dates and the times and type of transportation youwill need.
  • 4. Expect the Unexpected. The rainy season starts in late May-June. Hurricane seaosn runs from June to November. There will always be an accident or slow down depending on the time of day. Trust the professionals at A Regal Transportation Services to stay on top of the traffic reports to get your group to your destinations safely
